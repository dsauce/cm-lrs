## Green Bond Framework — eligible categories

Net proceeds (an amount equal thereto) are to be allocated to:

1. **Renewable energy generation** — onshore and offshore wind, solar PV.
2. **Energy efficiency** — transmission and distribution upgrades with documented loss-reduction ≥10%.
3. **Clean transport infrastructure** — EV charging networks of ≥50 kW per unit and rail electrification.
4. **Green hydrogen** — electrolyser capacity powered ≥90% by renewable sources on an annual look-back basis.

A second-party opinion has been obtained from a recognised ESG rating provider (to be named in the final prospectus).

## Covenant package (summary)

### Incurrence covenants

The Issuer may not incur additional financial indebtedness unless, after giving pro forma effect:

- **Net Debt to EBITDA** is no greater than **5.00x**, where EBITDA is adjusted to add back start-up losses from greenfield renewable assets for a period of 24 months from commercial operation date; and
- **Net Debt to RCF** (recurring cash flow from operating assets, defined in the indenture) is no greater than **6.50x**.

### Permitted debt baskets

The incurrence test does not apply to:

- (a) A super senior revolving credit facility of up to **EUR 150m**;
- (b) Project finance debt at the level of any operating SPV, capped at **EUR 750m in aggregate**, provided such debt is non-recourse to the Issuer (limited recourse for sponsor support obligations not exceeding 15% of project debt is permitted);
- (c) Acquired debt in respect of an acquired business, capped at **EUR 50m per transaction**;
- (d) Hedging in the ordinary course;
- (e) A general basket of **EUR 75m** at any time outstanding.

### Restricted payments

The Issuer may not make restricted payments unless:

1. No event of default is continuing;
2. Net Debt to EBITDA after giving pro forma effect is no greater than **4.25x**;
3. Restricted payments since the issue date do not exceed **a starter basket of EUR 25m** plus **50% of consolidated net income** since the issue date.

### Change of control

Upon a change of control, each noteholder may require the Issuer to repurchase its notes at **101%** plus accrued interest.

"Change of control" means any person or group of persons acting in concert (other than persons that are members of the existing controlling shareholder group identified in the prospectus, or any pension fund or sovereign wealth fund) acquiring directly or indirectly more than **50%** of the voting rights in the Issuer.

### Negative pledge

The Issuer and Material Subsidiaries shall not create or permit security over their assets to secure financial indebtedness, other than:

- Security on assets at the level of project SPVs (consistent with the permitted project finance basket);
- Security existing at the issue date;
- Tax, statutory and bank-account security in the ordinary course;
- A general basket of **EUR 30m** in aggregate.

### Asset disposal

The Issuer may dispose of any asset for fair market value, provided that net cash proceeds from such disposal in excess of **EUR 20m** in any 12-month period are applied within 365 days to (i) reinvestment in Eligible Green Projects, (ii) prepayment of permitted senior debt, or (iii) tender of the notes at par.

**Equity claw:** none (consistent with senior unsecured structure).

## Information undertakings

- Annual audited consolidated financial statements within 120 days of fiscal year-end;
- Interim unaudited reports within 90 days of half-year and 60 days of Q1 and Q3;
- Annual Green Bond Reporting within 6 months of fiscal year-end, including allocation of proceeds and impact metrics (MWh generated, tCO₂e avoided, electrolyser capacity online);
- Compliance certificate with each set of financials;
- Notice of any change of control event or event of default within 5 business days.

## Events of default

Standard Nordic HY package, including:

1. Non-payment (5 business day grace period for interest);
2. Cross-acceleration to financial indebtedness exceeding **EUR 25m**, excluding non-recourse project finance debt;
3. Insolvency events affecting the Issuer;
4. Breach of incurrence or restricted-payments covenant uncured for 30 days after written notice;
5. Loss of all operating licences for any subsidiary contributing >25% of group EBITDA, uncured for 90 days;
6. Invalidity of the notes documentation.
