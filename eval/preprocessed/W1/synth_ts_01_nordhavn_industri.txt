## Security package

The notes will be secured by first-priority security interests over:

1. **Share pledges** — all shares in each material subsidiary (defined as any subsidiary contributing >5% to group EBITDA or representing >5% of total assets, tested semi-annually).
2. **Intra-group receivables** — assignment of all intra-group loans exceeding SEK 50m advanced by the Issuer or any Guarantor.
3. **Bank account pledges** — all bank accounts of the Issuer and Guarantors holding aggregate balances exceeding SEK 25m, excluding ordinary-course operating accounts.
4. **Floating charges** — Swedish business mortgages ("företagsinteckningar") up to SEK 1,500m over the operating assets of each Swedish Guarantor.

The security agent holds the security on behalf of the noteholders under a parallel debt structure governed by Swedish law.

## Covenant package (summary)

### Incurrence covenants

The Issuer and Guarantors may not, and shall not permit any subsidiary to, incur additional financial indebtedness unless, after giving pro forma effect to such incurrence, the **Net Interest-Bearing Debt to EBITDA ratio** is no greater than:

- **3.50x** at any time on or before the second anniversary of the issue date; and
- **3.25x** at any time thereafter.

EBITDA is calculated on a last-twelve-months basis with synergies up to 25% of run-rate cost savings permitted to be added back, subject to a 18-month look-forward cap.

### Permitted debt baskets

The incurrence test does not apply to:

- (a) Refinancing of existing indebtedness on a substantially similar basis;
- (b) A super senior revolving credit facility of up to **SEK 750m** (or its equivalent), ranking senior to the notes solely with respect to enforcement proceeds;
- (c) Hedging obligations entered into in the ordinary course of business;
- (d) Capital leases up to **SEK 200m** in aggregate;
- (e) Acquired debt in respect of bolt-on acquisitions, capped at **SEK 100m per transaction** and **SEK 300m in aggregate**;
- (f) A general basket of **SEK 150m** at any time outstanding.

### Restricted payments

The Issuer may not (and shall procure that no Guarantor or subsidiary shall) make any restricted payment (defined to include dividends, distributions, share buybacks, voluntary prepayment of subordinated debt) unless:

1. No event of default is continuing;
2. After giving pro forma effect, Net Interest-Bearing Debt to EBITDA is no greater than **3.00x**;
3. The aggregate amount of restricted payments made since the issue date does not exceed **50% of consolidated net income** since the issue date, plus a starter basket of **SEK 50m**.

### Change of control

Upon a change of control event, each noteholder has the right (but not the obligation) to require the Issuer to repurchase its notes at **101%** of principal plus accrued interest. "Change of control" is defined as any person or group of persons acting in concert (other than the existing controlling shareholders identified in the prospectus) acquiring more than **50%** of the voting rights in Nordhavn Group AB.

### Negative pledge

Neither the Issuer nor any Guarantor will create or permit to subsist any security interest over any of its assets, other than:

- Security securing the notes;
- Security existing on the issue date and disclosed in the prospectus;
- Security arising by operation of law in the ordinary course of business;
- Security on after-acquired property to the extent existing at acquisition;
- Other security up to a general basket of **SEK 75m** in aggregate.

## Events of default (summary)

Standard Nordic HY package, including:

1. Non-payment (5 business day grace period for interest; no grace period for principal);
2. Cross-acceleration to financial indebtedness exceeding **SEK 100m**;
3. Insolvency events affecting the Issuer or any Material Guarantor;
4. Breach of incurrence test or restricted payments covenant uncured for 30 days after notice;
5. Cessation of business of the Issuer or any Material Guarantor;
6. Invalidity or repudiation of the notes, guarantees or security.

## Risk factors (representative, abbreviated)

- Cyclical exposure to industrial end-markets in the Nordics, Germany and the UK.
- Concentration risk: top five customers contribute approximately 38% of group revenue.
- Refinancing risk on the proposed SEK 750m super senior revolving credit facility (maturing one year before the notes).
- Foreign exchange risk: approximately 45% of revenue is EUR-denominated whilst all debt is SEK-denominated post-issuance.
