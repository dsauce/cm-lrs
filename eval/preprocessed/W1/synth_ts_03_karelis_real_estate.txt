## Security package

The notes will be secured by first-priority security over:

1. **Mortgage security** over the identified initial property portfolio comprising 14 commercial properties in Denmark (8) and southern Sweden (6), with an aggregate appraised market value of approximately EUR 580m as at the most recent independent valuation (Q1 2026, illustrative);
2. **Share pledges** over all shares in each propco Guarantor;
3. **Assignment of rental income** under all qualifying lease agreements where annual contracted rent exceeds EUR 250,000;
4. **Bank account pledges** over rent collection accounts and reserve accounts.

The Security Agent shall obtain updated independent valuations of the secured portfolio at intervals not exceeding 18 months.

## Covenant package (summary)

### Financial covenants (incurrence basis)

The Issuer may not incur additional financial indebtedness unless, after pro forma effect:

- **Loan-to-Value (LTV)** of the consolidated portfolio is no greater than **55%**;
- **Secured LTV** (relating only to the notes and any other secured creditors of the Issuer or Guarantors) is no greater than **45%**;
- **Net Interest Coverage Ratio (ICR)** is at least **1.75x**, calculated on a last-twelve-months basis with stabilised income from acquired or developed assets included from the date of practical completion or contractual rent commencement, whichever is later.

LTV is calculated using the most recent independent valuation, refreshed at least annually and immediately following any acquisition or disposal exceeding 5% of consolidated portfolio value.

### Maintenance covenants

Tested on each Reference Date (each interest payment date) on a last-twelve-months basis:

- LTV ≤ **60%** at any time (failure constitutes an event of default after 30-day cure);
- ICR ≥ **1.50x**;
- Liquidity (cash plus undrawn committed credit facilities) ≥ **EUR 25m** at all times.

### Permitted debt baskets

In addition to the notes, the Issuer and Guarantors may incur:

- (a) A super senior revolving credit facility of up to **EUR 100m**, ranking senior to the notes solely in enforcement;
- (b) Mortgage debt at the level of any propco Guarantor up to **70% LTV at the property level** and **EUR 250m in aggregate**, provided such debt is non-recourse to the Issuer (except for customary sponsor support);
- (c) Acquired debt assumed in connection with property acquisitions, capped at **EUR 50m per acquisition** and **EUR 150m in aggregate**, subject to the consolidated LTV remaining ≤ 55% pro forma;
- (d) Hedging in the ordinary course;
- (e) A general basket of **EUR 50m** at any time outstanding.

### Restricted payments

The Issuer may not make restricted payments unless:

1. No event of default is continuing;
2. Consolidated LTV is no greater than **50%** pro forma;
3. ICR is at least **2.00x** pro forma;
4. Aggregate restricted payments since the issue date do not exceed **a starter basket of EUR 15m** plus **50% of consolidated funds from operations (FFO)** since the issue date.

### Change of control

Upon a change of control of the Issuer or Karelis Property Holding A/S, each noteholder may put its notes back to the Issuer at **101%** plus accrued interest.

"Change of control" means any person or group of persons acting in concert (other than the existing controlling shareholder identified in the prospectus and its affiliates) acquiring directly or indirectly more than **50%** of the voting rights in Karelis Property Holding A/S or the Issuer; or the existing controlling shareholder ceasing to hold at least **30%** of the voting rights in Karelis Property Holding A/S.

### Negative pledge

Standard real-estate negative pledge with carve-outs for:

- Mortgage security at the propco level (consistent with permitted mortgage debt basket);
- Statutory and bank account security in the ordinary course;
- A general basket of **EUR 20m** in aggregate.

### Asset disposal

Disposals of secured properties are permitted, subject to:

- Application of net cash proceeds within 365 days to (i) acquisition of replacement properties of equivalent value, (ii) prepayment of permitted secured debt at par, or (iii) tender of the notes at the then-prevailing call price; and
- The consolidated LTV remaining ≤ 55% pro forma.

## Information undertakings

- Annual audited consolidated financial statements within 120 days of fiscal year-end;
- Interim unaudited financials within 60 days of each quarter-end;
- Quarterly property-level reporting: occupancy, weighted average lease term (WALT), top-10 tenant exposure;
- Independent portfolio valuation at least annually;
- Compliance certificate with each set of financials;
- Notice of any change of control event or event of default within 5 business days.

---

## Events of default

1. Non-payment (5 business day grace period for interest);
2. Breach of maintenance covenant (LTV, ICR, liquidity) uncured for 30 days after notice;
3. Cross-acceleration to financial indebtedness exceeding **EUR 25m**, excluding non-recourse mortgage debt;
4. Insolvency of the Issuer or any Material Guarantor;
5. Loss of mortgage security on assets representing more than 10% of secured portfolio value;
6. Final, non-appealable judgment exceeding **EUR 15m**;
7. Invalidity of notes documentation, guarantees or security.

## Risk factors (representative, abbreviated)

- Property valuation risk: portfolio yields sensitive to commercial real-estate market conditions in Denmark and southern Sweden.
- Tenant concentration: top 10 tenants account for approximately 42% of contracted rental income.
- Refinancing risk on the proposed EUR 100m super senior revolver maturing one year before the notes.
- Floating-rate exposure on the notes against partially fixed-rate lease income (mitigated by 7-year average lease length on the office portfolio).
